Privacy Policy

Aanalog, LLC builds creative software for analog film, camera emulation, and computational media.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website, apps, and related services.

Information We Collect

Website

Our website is a static informational site. We do not ask you to create an account on the website or submit personal information through a web form.

Support Email

If you contact us for support, we receive the information you choose to include, such as your email address, device model, app version, iOS version, and description of the issue.

Apps

Our apps may request device permissions such as camera, photo library, microphone, or notification access when those permissions are needed for app features. Your videos, audio, and images stay on your device and are not uploaded to Aanalog servers.

How We Use Information

We use support information to answer questions, troubleshoot issues, improve our apps, and maintain records needed to operate the business.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when needed to provide support, comply with legal obligations, protect rights and safety, or operate services with vendors who process information on our behalf.

Your Choices

You can control app permissions, such as camera, photo library, microphone, and notification access, through your device settings.

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information you have provided to us, subject to legal and technical limitations.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Because your videos, audio, and images remain on your device and are not uploaded to our servers, protecting your device and backups is also important.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and post the revised policy on our website.
