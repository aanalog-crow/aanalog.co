Support

For support with Aaron8mm, AaronCam, or another Aanalog app, email us at support@aanalog.co.

What to include

Please include the details that help us understand and reproduce the issue.

  • The app name
  • Your device model
  • Your iOS version
  • The app version
  • A brief description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem, if possible

Aaron8mm

Aaron8mm is a Super 8 camera for iPhone with cartridge-first recording, on-device film development, color and black-and-white capture, and optional pro controls.

AaronCam

AaronCam is a videotube app for iPhone. Its analog effects are powered by patent-pending signal processing technology designed to model the behavior of older imaging systems.

Privacy

Your videos, audio, and images stay entirely on your own device. They are not uploaded to Aanalog servers.

For more information, see our Privacy Policy.
