Camera tools for analog video.

Aanalog apps use signal processing and purpose-built interfaces to make iPhone footage behave like older image systems.

Videotube Camera

AaronCam

AaronCam is an iPhone videotube camera built around the strange, beautiful behavior of pre-digital imaging.

Its analog effects are powered by patent-pending signal processing designed to model the color, motion, and texture of early home video cameras.

Super 8 Camera

Aaron8mm

Aaron8mm is a tactile Super 8 camera for iPhone with cartridge-first recording, film door interactions, color and black-and-white capture, and optional pro controls.

Camera, microphone, and film development stay on device unless you choose to save or share the developed video.
